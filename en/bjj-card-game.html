BJJ Card Game

A martial arts game about position, pressure, and submissions

ChainGrapplers turns the flow of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu into a tactical two-player card game. Build positional chains, attack when the timing is right, and defend when your opponent creates submission pressure.

For young grapplers

Bring the academy home

Colors and positions help kids recognize how control, defense, and submissions connect without turning the game into a lesson.

For families

No BJJ experience required

Parents, grandparents, and friends can play by following the color chain. Knowledge of BJJ adds context, but the rules carry the game.

For academies

A quick game between sessions

Two teammates can play short matches and discuss the choices that built or broke each positional chain.

BJJ Gift

A gift that gets played

Common BJJ gifts include clothing, tape, and training equipment. ChainGrapplers is an alternative for someone who already has their gi or rash guard and wants something social, tactical, and connected to the sport.

It can work as a birthday gift, holiday gift, academy prize, or present for a new student. People who do not train can still play, making it easier for a grappler to share their interest with family.

How it works

From neutral position to the finish

Each standard card has a starting color and a result color. The next card must begin where the previous card ended. Defense pulls the exchange toward more neutral positions, while attacks build pressure toward orange and red. A red attack creates a submission threat that must be answered with an escape.

Frequently asked questions

About the game

Do you need to know BJJ?

No. The colors show which cards connect. Knowledge of the sport makes the positions more familiar but is not required.

Is it a game for kids?

It is designed to be easy to begin. Younger children may need help in their first rounds, while the tactical choices provide more depth as players learn.

Can you try it first?

Yes. The browser demo is free and demonstrates the core game flow.

How many people play?

Each match is played by two players.
